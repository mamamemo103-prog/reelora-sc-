Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · Effective date: 1 March 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to Reelora at reelora.netlify.app (the “site”). It describes how we handle information when you browse pages, open games, or contact us. This version replaces earlier drafts and reflects our practices as of the effective date above.

1. Who is responsible

Reelora operates the site, which offers lightweight browser games for adults. We act as the controller of personal data described here, unless a separate notice says otherwise for a specific feature.

2. What you can do here

The site is built for casual play: games load in your browser, usually in a new tab. We do not require registration. You may leave at any time; closing your browser or clearing storage may remove some locally stored preferences.

3. Data we may process

Depending on how you use the site, we may process:

  • Device and connection data — browser name and version, device type, operating system, language, referrer, rough engagement signals (e.g. pages viewed, session length), and similar technical metadata.
  • Preference and security data — information stored in cookies or local storage (for example age-confirmation choices, cookie-banner decisions, or settings needed for the site to work).
  • Messages you send us — name, email address, subject, and message body if you use the contact form or email support@reelora.netlify.app.

Individual games may include their own scripts (for example analytics). Where that happens, those providers’ policies also apply to what they collect.

4. Why we use data

We process information to:

  • Deliver pages and games and keep them secure and reliable;
  • Remember essential preferences (cookies, age gate, cookie consent);
  • Measure aggregate traffic and fix errors so we can improve layout and performance;
  • Reply to support and policy enquiries;
  • Meet legal obligations and protect Reelora, users, and the public where appropriate.

5. Legal bases (where they matter)

If laws such as the UK or EU GDPR apply, we rely on: contract / steps at your request (running the site you asked to use), legitimate interests (secure operation, product improvement, limited analytics), consent (where required for non-essential cookies or similar), and legal obligation when we must comply with law.

6. Cookies and local storage

We use essential cookies/local storage to keep core features working (for example navigation preferences, age gate, and security checks). Optional cookies may be used for analytics and measurement tools only after consent where required.

7. Processors and sharing

We use trusted hosting, infrastructure, and analytics partners who process data only for defined service purposes. We do not sell your personal information. We may disclose information if required by law, to respond to lawful requests, or to protect rights and safety.

8. How long we keep data

We retain data only as long as needed for the purposes above or as the law requires. Server logs and security records are kept for limited periods. Contact messages may be stored to handle follow-up and records unless you ask us to delete them and we have no overriding reason to keep them.

9. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data, to object to certain processing, to revoke consent, or to complain to a supervisory authority. To exercise a right, contact us via Contact or support@reelora.netlify.app with a clear description of your request. We may need to confirm your identity.

10. Age

Reelora is intended for visitors 18+. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has contacted us, please write to us so we can take appropriate steps.

11. International processing

Visitors may access the site from many countries. Data may be processed on servers or by providers in regions with different privacy laws. We use safeguards we consider appropriate for the type of data and processing involved.

12. Changes

We may update this Policy to reflect new features, partners, or legal requirements. We will revise the “Last updated” date when we publish changes. Material changes may be highlighted on the site. Continued use after the effective date means you accept the updated Policy.

13. Contact

Privacy questions: Contact page or support@reelora.netlify.app.
